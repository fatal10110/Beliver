# Marketing & Operations

## 1. Marketing Strategy
**Goal**: Build a dedicated niche audience of strategy/history buffs.

### Channels
1.  **Influencers**: Target YouTubers/Streamers who play Civilization, Crusader Kings, Age of Empires.
2.  **Community Hubs**:
    *   **Discord**: Central hub for feedback, lore discussion, LFG.
    *   **Reddit**: r/StrategyGames, r/4X.
3.  **Content Marketing**:
    *   **Dev Blogs**: "Deep dive into the Law System."
    *   **Lore Shorts**: 30-second videos explaining the backstory of Abrim.

## 2. Live Operations (LiveOps)
**Goal**: Retain players long-term.

### Cadence
*   **Weekly**: Rotation of "Featured Faction" (small XP bonus).
*   **Monthly**: Balance Patch (buff underused units).
*   **Quarterly**: **Seasons** (New Battle Pass, Reset Leaderboard, New Narrative Event).

### Community Management
*   **Moderation**: Strict anti-toxicity tools in chat. "Honor" system for good sportsmanship.
*   **Feedback Loop**: Monthly "State of the Game" post addressing top community concerns.

## 3. Localization
*   **Launch Languages**: English.
*   **Fast Follow**: Spanish, French, German, Portuguese (high strategy player bases).
*   **Process**: External translation agency + Community crowdsourcing for lore flavor text.
