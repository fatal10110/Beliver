# Marketing & Operations

## 1. Marketing Strategy
**Goal**: Build a dedicated niche audience of strategy, history, and prompt-engineering enthusiasts.

### Channels
1.  **Influencers**: YouTubers/Streamers who play Civilization, Crusader Kings, Age of Empires, and AI tooling.
2.  **Community Hubs**:
    *   **Discord**: Central hub for feedback, lore discussion, doctrine sharing.
    *   **Reddit**: r/StrategyGames, r/4X, r/PromptEngineering.
3.  **Content Marketing**:
    *   **Dev Blogs**: "How the Doctrine Compiler works" and "Law System deep dive."
    *   **Lore Shorts**: 30-second videos explaining Abrim's backstory.
    *   **Doctrine Spotlights**: Highlight top policy hashes and player sermons.

## 2. Live Operations (LiveOps)
**Goal**: Retain players long-term via seasons and meta evolution.

### Cadence
*   **Weekly**: Featured doctrine archetype or faction (small XP bonus).
*   **Monthly**: Balance patch (buff underused units).
*   **Quarterly**: **Seasons** (new battle pass, reset leaderboard, new narrative event).

### Community Management
*   **Moderation**: Strict anti-toxicity tools in chat and Oracle Feed.
*   **Feedback Loop**: Monthly "State of the Game" post addressing top community concerns.

## 3. Localization
*   **Launch Languages**: English.
*   **Fast Follow**: Spanish, French, German, Portuguese.
*   **Process**: External translation agency + community crowdsourcing for lore flavor text.
